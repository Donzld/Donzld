Tips for Growing Healthy Broccoli

Broccoli is a cool-season crop that can be very productive. Here are some tips:

  • Climate: Broccoli prefers cooler temperatures. Plant in spring or fall.
  • Sunlight: It needs full sun, at least 6 hours per day.
  • Soil: Rich, well-drained soil is best. Add compost before planting.
  • Watering: Water regularly, especially as the heads are forming.
