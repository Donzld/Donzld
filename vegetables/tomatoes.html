Tips for Growing Healthy Tomatoes

Tomatoes are a popular crop for many farmers. Here are some tips for a successful harvest:

  • Sunlight: Tomatoes need at least 6-8 hours of direct sunlight per day.
  • Soil: They prefer well-drained, fertile soil with a pH between 6.0 and 6.8.
  • Watering: Water deeply and regularly, especially during dry periods. Avoid overhead watering to prevent disease.
  • Spacing: Space plants about 2-3 feet apart to ensure good air circulation.
