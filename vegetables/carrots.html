Tips for Growing Healthy Carrots

Carrots are a versatile and rewarding crop. Here are some tips for a successful harvest:

  • Soil: Carrots need loose, sandy soil to grow straight. Remove rocks and clumps.
  • Sowing: Sow seeds thinly in rows, about 1/2 inch deep.
  • Watering: Keep the soil consistently moist to prevent cracking.
  • Thinning: Thin seedlings to about 1-2 inches apart to give them room to grow.
